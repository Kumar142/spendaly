Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Legend:
Mandatory — legally binding, non-negotiable
Good to have — strongly recommended
Informational — context and disclosures

1. Data We Collect Mandatory

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense records — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.
  • Usage data — basic logs of app interactions used to diagnose errors and improve the service.

We do not collect payment card details, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data Mandatory

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To authenticate your account and display your personalised expense history.
  • To generate summaries, category breakdowns, and spending insights shown in the app.
  • To send occasional service-related notifications (e.g., account activity alerts).
  • We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

3. Data Storage Informational

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard measures to protect it against unauthorised access, including:

  • Encrypted storage of passwords (we never store plaintext passwords).
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure.
  • Data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

4. Third-Party Services Informational

Spendly may rely on the following third-party services to operate:

  • Hosting providers — to store and serve the application.
  • Analytics tools — only aggregated, anonymised data is shared; no personally identifiable information is transmitted.
  • We do not integrate with any payment processors or bank APIs that would access your financial accounts.

Each third-party service is governed by its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Your Rights Mandatory

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request that your account and all associated data be permanently deleted.
  • Portability — request your expense data in a machine-readable format.

6. Contact Us Good to have

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the contact details provided in the app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 7 business days.
